Problems I Faced During the CEH Practical Exam:

  • Room with No Disturbance: Ensure you have a quiet room without interruptions.
  • Internet Speed: A good internet speed of at least 30 Mbps is crucial because the exam machine can be slow, especially when running heavy tasks.
  • No Direct Copy-Paste: You cannot copy or paste commands or notes directly from your PC or Google search to the exam machine. This restriction wasted a lot of my time.
  • Service Password Login: I correctly cracked a service password but couldn‚Äôt log in. Once logged in, the system automatically remembered the first user‚Äôs credentials, making it impossible to log in as a second user.
  • Remember Password Option: If you mistakenly check the ‚Äòremember password‚Äô option, you might face login issues. Ensure to uncheck it if ticked by mistake.
  • RDP Login: I cracked the RDP password and tried connecting using the Remmina tool, but I couldn‚Äôt log in even with the correct username and password.
  • Complex Questions: Some questions were not understandable at first glance. It is important to read them peacefully and ensure you understand them correctly before attempting to answer.
  • Sqlmap Issues: In my case, Sqlmap did not work as expected. Do not rely solely on Sqlmap; have backup tools like JSQL or Ghauri ready for SQL injection tasks.
