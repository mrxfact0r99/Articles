The bug I found is an information disclosure. I enumerated target.com's subdomains and found one subdomain, let's assume it is "xyz.target.com", that showed a timestamp in milliseconds. I felt weird, so I ran Dirsearch on that domain.

Tip: Always fuzz those domains where you see anything weird or nothing at all.

I ran Dirsearch and found an error code 400 in "xyz.target.com/servlet/%C0%AE%C0%AE%C0%AF". Dirsearch is a powerful tool that fuzzes with encodings, and it showed some internal paths, IP addresses, and codes. So, I reported it as an information disclosure, and after four days, the company replied to me, "Your bug has been accepted, and we will reward you a bounty of 5,000 Indian rupees."
